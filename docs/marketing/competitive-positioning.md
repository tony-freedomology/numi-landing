## Zoe vs. YouVersion Bible App

### What YouVersion Does Well
- Massive library of Bible translations
- Huge selection of reading plans (thousands)
- Community features (share verses, see what friends are reading)
- Free (ad-supported or donation model)
- Extremely polished app

### Where Zoe is Different
- **YouVersion is a library. Zoe is an interactive discipleship tool.**
- YouVersion gives you content to consume. Zoe walks with you through your day.
- YouVersion doesn't remember you. If you miss a day, it just waits. Zoe follows up: "You mentioned wanting to read James this week. Did you get to it?"
- YouVersion's plans are generic. Zoe weaves in your church's sermon series.
- YouVersion is app-based. Zoe is SMS (no app needed, no login friction).

### When to Choose YouVersion Over Zoe
- You want a Bible reference tool (Zoe isn't trying to be a Bible app)
- You love browsing plans and devotionals (Zoe gives you one personalized rhythm, not a catalog)
- You want to see what your friends are reading (Zoe is private and personal)

### When to Choose Zoe Over YouVersion
- You're tired of starting plans and never finishing them
- You want someone who remembers your story
- You want your pastor's teaching to extend beyond Sunday
- You want day-long devotion, not just a morning reading

## The Switching Cost (Zoe's Moat)

The longer you use Zoe, the more it knows your story.

- Zoe remembers what you're struggling with
- Zoe remembers what you're praying for
- Zoe remembers who you're walking with (your pastor, small group, friend)
- Zoe remembers your commitments and follows up

Switching from Zoe to another app means:
- Losing all that relational memory
- Starting over with someone who doesn't know you
- Going back to generic content

**This is Zoe's moat.** Other apps can copy features. They can't copy your history with Zoe.
